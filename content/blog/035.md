<i>Mushkil hain zabas kalaam mera ai dil,<br>
sun sun ke ise sukhanwaraan-e-kaamil,<br>
aasaan kahne ki karte hain farmaish,<br>
"goim mushkil wa gar na goim mushkil."</i>

Too hard to grasp is my verse, O heart!<br>
Hearing it, the connoisseurs of art<br>
For a simpler style do ask.<br>
"Difficult, if I write, difficult, if do not."<br>
